# Gaussian Processes and Bayesian Optimization
Short tutorials and projects in the journey of understanding and mastering GP and BO

# Contents
The `GaussianProcessesPractice.ipynb` notebook contains a simple case of aplication of a GP trying to fit a sinusoidal function. Apart from that, it also includes the implementation of several types of basic kernels along with the computation of the correlation matrix through them in order to study their properties and dependence of hyperparameters. Finally, a simple case of kernel superposition is explored.
